Sarah Kim currently works as a data technologist at Pandora in Oakland and enjoys learning new technologies and consuming an unhealthy amount of almond croissants. The author begs forgiveness for her indiscriminate uses definite articles, as this is the habit of those who claim Korea as their motherland. An avid fan of the opera and of fine art, her banishment to the East Bay has left her no choice but to enjoy such hoity-toity pleasures through the glossy glare of her laptop screen. Sarah is the author of no books and lives with neither cats nor dogs at her apartment in Oakland.
